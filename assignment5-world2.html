Our Iranian government though, tried to deny this, by saying the crash happened because of engine failure, but after a few days, they had to come out with the truth, as the Canadian government started to find out the truth. So that the Iranian government could save them selves and not look bad, they made an apology and said the truth.

If the government of Canada had not interfered, the Iranian's would have not got the real truth behind the plane crash. It is despicable, that the Iranian government would want to hide the truth. Here is the video proof that started circuling around.
